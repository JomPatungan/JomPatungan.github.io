Anime! We've all heard of this somehow somewhere, right? When I was a kid, I used to love things like pokemon and minecraft, pretty standard stuff. I used to think anime was something overdramatic, and for non-sensical emos (which it probably still is in some cases), but one day my friend recommended me a game to play. It was an anime game? I was a bit generous at the time and I was fine at the idea that this would be my first interaction with the world of anime, and I can say that I will never regret that decision.



At first, anime was simply a concept I liked, but it started becoming something I love in every aspect. The first ever anime I watched was Sword Art Online. Though not really an anime I recommend, it still means a lot to me as it is the anime that brought me into the community itself. From there I started talking to my friends about anime-related things, play anime games, and obviously, watch anime. After a year or 2, I switched from the action genre to things like romance and relaxing themes, which I definitely enjoyed. Some good ones are Kaguya-sama, and Horimiya.




Fun fact, haikyuu is the only sports anime I've ever watched. I'm not really a sporty guy, so sports animes dont interest me. However haikyuu is an exception, since it's just amazing on it's own.

Anime is served a big role in my life. My inspiration at times, my entertainment, the emotions and memories it gave me, and the friends it led me to. I love anime, and I hope that never changes.
