   It's your Shooting Star, your Diamond in the rough, Hoshimachi Suisei!


Not the maid that hides and wait, but the prince that get things done. Hoshimachi Suisei is a virtual idol that dreams of performing live in Tokyo. Though behind this starry dream, lies a melancholic path that she had to take herself, before she could be where she is now.

Rewind to 2018-2019, she was an independent content creator that loved singing and dreamed to be an "idol". In order to get closer to her dream, she had auditioned for multitudes of companies, with multitudes of painful rejetions. There were multiple times where she had planned on stopping, yet she kept pursuing her goal. One day, she was finally accepted, and this was the turning point of her career.

Hololive is a virtual-talent agency that consists of virtual youtubers under the government of a japanese company. Today, hololive is the most popular Virtual group known in the internet, and this same company is what Suisei got accepted in. However, she wasn't completely accepted. At first she was hired as a music production group under hololive, but due to her constant activity and hardwork, she was then eventually accepted as a real member. Fast forward to now, she reached 1 million subscribers, released her first ever album, and even performed live on stage! All of these were just a dream, and to think that it has come to reality is moving, as someone who follows her progress. (TLDR: I am a huge fan of her.)
